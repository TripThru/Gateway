This is the TripThru Gateway API documention and Sandbox test framework

Please email us at support@tripthru.com to optain an api_key

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Use the GET /partners service to get a list of simulated partners and their coverage areas.

Once you know the areas covered by our partners, you can dispatch pickup requests to them via the POST /dispatch service.

Dispatching to an area not covered by any of our partners will result in a reject. Alternatively, you can get a list of quotes via

GET /quotes, and then dispatch to the partner of your choice. After dispatching, you can get the status of your trip via GET /tripstatus.

To get a list of all active trips use GET /trips.
