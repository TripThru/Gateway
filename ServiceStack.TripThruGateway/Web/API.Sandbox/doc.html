This is the TripThru Gateway API documention and Sandbox test framework

Please email us at support@tripthru.com to optain an api_key

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Sandbox is currently integrated with many simulated partners and a few T Dispatch demo cab offices

You may download the T Dispatch driver app and login as TripThruDemoDriver password TripThru

You may then log into our T Dispatch demo cab office "sanfran" controller "TripThruDemo" password TripThruDemo

You can use the /partners services to get a list of simulated partners and their coverage areas. The T Dispatch sanfran office is one of them

Once you know the areas covered by our simulated partners, you can dispatch pickup requests to them via the /dispatch services

If you log into your driver app and dispatch to the sanfran office you may receive the booking through your T Dispatch driver app

Use the /status services to monitor the progress of your booking. The simulated partners function as any real partner would.

The routes are real and the ETAs, prices, and travel times realistic
